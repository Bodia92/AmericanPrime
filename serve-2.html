Farmers&Ranchers

See what’s happening in every field — without stepping into it.American Prime helps farmers monitor crop performance, detect issues earlier, and respond faster using satellite intelligence and precision drone applications.From crop health monitoring to targeted treatments, our technology helps you save time, reduce unnecessary inputs, and protect your yield potential.Know Your Fields BetterManaging modern farms means monitoring dozens—or hundreds—of fields across multiple locations.American Prime gives you a clear view of crop conditions across your entire operation.

With AP Intelligence you can:

  • Monitor crop health across all your fields

  • Identify crop stress before it spreads

  • Compare field performance across seasons

  • Detect nutrient deficiencies and pest risks

  • Track yield potential throughout the growing season

Reduce Scouting Time

Field scouting is time consuming and problems are often discovered too late.Satellite monitoring allows you to quickly identify which areas actually need attention, so you spend less time driving fields and more time acting on the right insights.

Act Quickly with Precision Drone Applications

Once an issue is detected, AP Drones allow you to deploy targeted treatments exactly where they are needed.Instead of treating entire fields, you can apply inputs only to affected areas, helping reduce:

  • Chemical use
  • Fertilizer waste
  • Operational costs
